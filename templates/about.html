About Us

Our story...

Smallgoods American Cheeses & Provisions got its start in the San Diego Farmer's Markets in November, 2015. In February 2020, just 3 weeks before Covid - we opened our brick-and-mortar shop. Lousy timing! But we're proud to have made it to the other side.

Our shop...

We're our version of a gourmet deli, cafe, caterer and some say we make the best sandwiches and charcuterie platters around. We're a husband and wife team +1 employee that exclusively specializes in American-made regional and seasonal cheeses, cured meats and domestic and local Farmer Market finds.

Our makers...

Our American makers are the best-of-the-best and deserve 100% of our commitment and our support - and that's what we've been doing for longer than any other retailer.
